One BackboneHow the signal flows

These aren't five gadgets, they're one operations platform wired around a shared work-management backbone. Detection flows into ticketing, field work flows into records, records flow back out to clients, and Tempo measures how well the whole thing gets used. I built them decoupled on purpose: a fault in one channel can never take the others down. That balance of integration and resilience is the part I'm proudest of.
