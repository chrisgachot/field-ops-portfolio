Systems ThinkingFive tools, one platform

Each app does one job well, but the value is in how they connect around a shared work-management backbone: detection flows into ticketing, field work flows into records, and records flow back out to clients.

I built them as decoupled, independent services on purpose, so a fault in one can never cascade into the others , while still sharing a common spine. That balance of integration and resilience is the part I'm proudest of.
